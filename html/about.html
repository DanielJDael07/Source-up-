ABOUT US

Crystal Tec is an IT Managed Service Provider specializing in Windows server, Network maintenance, Cyber Security and
Cloud Computing Hosted Services.

The company has been operating since 2002 and our offices are located in Bayron Bay & Gold Coast.

At Crystal Tec, we can provide your business with the latest Information Technology solutions to help your business grow. We are committed to providing you with only the best technology and the right solution so you can achieve your goals and realize your business’ full potential.

With our IT Managed Service, you do not have to worry about taking care of your company’s IT infrastructure. We have the expertise to handle it for you, so you can focus on what you do best growing your business.
